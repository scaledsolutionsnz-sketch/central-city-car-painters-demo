What We Do

Bodywork & paint, done properly

From a carpark scuff to a full insurance rebuild, every job goes through prep, paint and finish the same careful way.

01

Collision & Panel Repair

Bumps, dings and accident damage straightened, replaced and refinished back to pre-crash condition.

02

Full & Partial Resprays

Tired, faded or recoloured paintwork brought back to a deep, even gloss across the whole car or a single panel.

03

Scratch & Scuff Repair

Key marks, trolley scrapes and door dings cut back, blended and polished so you'd never know they were there.

04

Dent & Ding Removal

Small dents pulled and reshaped — often without repainting — to keep your original factory finish intact.

05

Rust Repair & Treatment

Rust cut out, panels repaired and protected so the problem doesn't bubble back through your fresh paint.

06

Insurance & Assessments

We'll quote your claim, talk to the assessor and manage the repair end to end — major insurers welcome.

About Us

A Dunedin paint shop that takes pride in the finish

Central City Car Painters is a local panel and paint workshop looking after Dunedin drivers — everyday runabouts, work utes and the cars people love. Run hands-on by Vinnie, we keep the team small so the person quoting your job is the person who stands behind it.

We'd rather do it once and do it right. That means proper prep, the correct paint match and an honest chat about what your car actually needs before any spray gun comes out.
