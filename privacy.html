Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This is placeholder content for demonstration purposes and should be reviewed before publication.

1. Our commitment

Central City Car Painters respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information in line with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).

2. What we collect

We only collect what we need to quote and carry out your work, including:

  • Your name, phone number and email address
  • Vehicle details and a description of the work required
  • Any photos or documents you choose to send us
  • Insurance and claim details where relevant

3. How we use it

Your information is used to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, carry out repairs, liaise with your insurer where applicable, and keep you updated on your job. We do not sell your information.

4. Sharing

We may share information with third parties only where it is needed to complete your work — for example, your insurer, an assessor, or a parts supplier. We require those parties to handle your information responsibly.

5. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to retain it only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law.

6. Website forms

Enquiry and feedback forms on this site are processed by a third-party form service to deliver your message to us by email. Only the details you enter are submitted.

7. Your rights

You may ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. Contact us using the details below and we'll help.

8. Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at centralcityltd2025@gmail.com or on 03 456 3848.
